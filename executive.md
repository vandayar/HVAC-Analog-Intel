EXECUTIVE BRIEF: Project HVAC-Intel
Target: Reliance Home Comfort Fleet Optimization

THE PROBLEM: The "Analog" Data Gap
70% of legacy HVAC units lack IoT connectivity, creating a "Diagnostic Black Hole." This leads to:

High Latency: 45+ minute manual lookups for blink codes.

Operational Leakage: Excessive "No Fault Found" (NFF) truck rolls.

THE SOLUTION: The "Shadow-IoT" Framework
HVAC-Intel is a zero-CapEx AI architecture using Gemini 2.5 Flash to turn a technician’s mobile camera into a high-precision diagnostic sensor.

Multimodal Detection: Real-time analysis of control board LED patterns.

Knowledge Retrieval: Instant cross-referencing of service manuals for exact part numbers.
