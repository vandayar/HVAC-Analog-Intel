# 🛠️ HVAC-Intel: AI-Powered Diagnostics for Legacy Infrastructure
**Bridging the "Analog-to-Digital" Gap in Field Service Operations**

## 📌 Executive Summary
A massive 70% of residential and commercial HVAC units are "Analog"—they lack IoT sensors and Wi-Fi connectivity. This creates a "Diagnostic Black Hole," leading to high labor costs, incorrect part orders, and excessive "truck rolls."

**HVAC-Intel** leverages **Gemini 2.5 Flash** to turn any technician's smartphone into a universal IoT sensor. By using Multimodal AI to "read" legacy blink codes and wiring, we extract 100% software-driven ROI without any hardware upgrades.

---

## 🚀 The Solution: "Technician-as-a-Sensor"
Traditional diagnostics require 30-45 minutes of manual lookup. HVAC-Intel reduces this to **under 5 minutes** through:

* **Multimodal Blink-Code Detection:** The tech snaps a photo/video of the control board; the AI identifies the fault pattern (e.g., 3 Red Flashes = Pressure Switch Failure).
* **Zero-CapEx Deployment:** No new sensors or IoT modules are required on the furnace or AC unit.
* **Dynamic Knowledge Retrieval:** The Agent cross-references the detected model with indexed service manuals to provide the exact part number and safety protocol.
